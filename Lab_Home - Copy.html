Welcome to Basingstoke Labour

The unofficial website of the Labour Party in Basingstoke. Here you will find information about local campaigns run by our local councillors on behalf of the local people. If you would like to get involved with the party in any way, delivering flyers, talking to other local people about issue that affect our town or apply to join as a Labour candidate, then please do not hesitate to get in touch through the contact us link above.
